Gear Insights and Tips: Elevate Your Drone Experience

When it comes to drone photography and FPV racing, having the right gear and knowing how to use it can make a world of difference. In this section, I'll share insights into the equipment I use and provide valuable tips to help you capture stunning photographs and excel in the exciting world of FPV racing.

Drone Photography Gear

For my drone photography adventures, I rely on my trusty DJI. Its camera, control, and battery life make it the perfect companion for capturing breathtaking landscapes and precious family moments.

FPV Racing Gear

FPV racing requires a different set of gear, designed to handle high speeds and tough competition. For my races, I don't use any specific brands. Instead, I build my own FPV drones. This approach not only allows me to tailor each drone to my specific needs but also makes it easier to fix them after a crash.
