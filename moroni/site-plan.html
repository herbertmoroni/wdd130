Home page

Welcome to Drone Photography and FPV Adventures!

Hi there! I'm Moroni, a software developer by profession, but a drone enthusiast at heart. This website is my little corner on the internet where I share my passion for capturing the world from above with drone photography, and the thrill of FPV (First Person View) drone racing.

Ever since I laid my hands on my first drone, I was hooked. There's something magical about seeing the world from a bird's eye view â€“ the landscapes, the horizons, and everything in between. It's like a different world up there, and I aim to share that beauty with you through my collection of stunning aerial photographs.

But that's not all! I'm also an avid FPV racer. There's a unique adrenaline rush that comes with piloting a drone at high speeds, maneuvering through obstacles, and competing with fellow racers. It's a world full of excitement and challenges, and I can't wait to bring you along for the ride.

So, whether you're here to appreciate the beauty of drone photography, learn a few tips and tricks of the trade, or simply share in the excitement of FPV racing, you're in the right place. Dive in, explore, and let's embark on this exciting journey together!

Gear & Tips

Gear Insights and Tips: Elevate Your Drone Experience

When it comes to drone photography and FPV racing, having the right gear and knowing how to use it can make a world of difference. In this section, I'll share insights into the equipment I use and provide valuable tips to help you capture stunning photographs and excel in the exciting world of FPV racing.

Drone Photography Gear:
For my drone photography adventures, I rely on my trusty DJI. Its camera, control, and battery life make it the perfect companion for capturing breathtaking landscapes and precious family moments.

FPV Racing Gear:
FPV racing requires a different set of gear, designed to handle high speeds and tough competition. For my races, I don't use any specific brands. Instead, I build my own FPV drones. This approach not only allows me to tailor each drone to my specific needs but also makes it easier to fix them after a crash.
